# **App Name**: ViMond Realty Showcase

## Core Features:

- Project Portfolio Display: Showcase all current and past real estate projects with high-quality images, key details, and location information (e.g., Vivencia, Signature Tower II, Verïsta).
- Company Narrative & Philosophy: Dedicated sections articulating ViMond Realty's 'Who We Are' statement, vision for 'Intelligent Luxury', and unique differentiators in the market.
- Client Testimonials & Feedback: Display authentic quotes and experiences from homeowners, highlighting satisfaction with ViMond properties and services.
- Blogs & Insights Section: A structured section to host informative articles and company news, allowing users to 'Learn More' and stay updated on real estate trends.
- Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ): A searchable or categorized list of common questions regarding development processes, property details, and purchasing options.
- Interactive Contact & Inquiry Form: A user-friendly form for potential clients to submit detailed inquiries, along with contact details and office address.
- AI-Powered Inquiry Assistant: A generative AI tool that can synthesize and extract key information from project descriptions, company philosophy, and FAQs to answer specific user questions in natural language.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, sophisticated blue (#2A4566), evoking trust, luxury, and modern stability. This color contrasts effectively with light backgrounds.
- Background color: A very light, almost white, cool gray (#EDF1F5), creating a clean and spacious feel, enhancing readability and emphasizing visual content.
- Accent color: A lively but refined cyan-blue (#4DB9CC), providing a fresh visual highlight for interactive elements and key calls-to-action, drawing attention without being overpowering.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, clean, and highly legible characteristics, suitable for conveying a professional and objective luxury brand image across all content types.
- Use minimalist, clean, and precise line icons that align with the brand's 'cutting-edge' and 'intelligent design' principles, maintaining a sophisticated and modern aesthetic.
- Emphasize a clean, spacious, and visually-driven layout with large hero sections for project highlights, distinct content blocks for readability, and abundant whitespace to convey elegance and premium quality.
- Incorporate subtle, smooth transition animations for section reveals, image carousels, and interactive elements to provide a polished and responsive user experience without distracting from content.
